## How to Add Your Music

1. **Find a romantic song** (MP3 format recommended)
2. **Rename it to:** `music.mp3`
3. **Place it in this folder** (`assets/music.mp3`)
4. The website will automatically play it when your crush clicks the music button!

### Recommended Romantic Songs:
- Any soft, romantic instrumental
- Your crush's favorite song
- A song that's meaningful to both of you
- Soft piano or acoustic versions work great!

### Where to Get Royalty-Free Music:
- YouTube Audio Library
- Pixabay Music
- Bensound
- Free Music Archive

**Note:** Make sure the file is named exactly `music.mp3` and placed in this `assets` folder!
